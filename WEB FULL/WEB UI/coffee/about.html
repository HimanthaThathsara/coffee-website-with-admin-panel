Our Vision

For Coffee to be Sri Lankan Third Place, whether it’s because of an amazing coffee experience, a special customer moment, our positive impact in the community, or being a great place for our partners to work, we want our Siren to evoke a memorable feeling of happiness.

To inspire and nurture the human spirit—one person, one cup and one neighbourhood at a time.

Our Purpose

We create moments of connection with our customers and stores worthy of being their Third Place, while driving profitable growth to build and sustain our future success. We develop our partners to be their very best, and we are a force for good in all the communities we touch.

Creating a culture of warmth and belonging, where everyone is welcome. Acting with courage, challenging the status quo and finding new ways to grow our company and each other

Who we are

Our Story

Every day, we go to work hoping to do two things: share great coffee with our friends and help make the world a little better. It was true when the first opened in 1971 and it’s just as true today.

Back then, the company was a single store in Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market. From just a narrow storefront, offered some of the world’s finest fresh-roasted whole bean coffees. The name, inspired by Moby Dick, evoked the romance of the high seas and the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.
